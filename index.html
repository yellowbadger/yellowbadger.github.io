Our Process

  • Checkpoints

    We believe in transparency. We want you to be informed every step of the way. So during and after each phase we include you in our internal checkpoint conversations so that your feedback can be used to make course corrections and any changes integrated as soon as possible.

  • 1

    First Contact

    The first step is our initial consultation where we try to learn as much as we can about your organization and create a "Wish List" of everything you would like.

    Checkpoint

  • 2

    Proof of Concept

    During the proof of concept phase we identify the core functionality for the project and produce a minimally functional implementation that demonstrates that the idea is viable and assumptions like level of effort and complexity are valid.

    Checkpoint

  • 3

    Refine Design

    After providing you with the proof of concept, we work through all its functional aspects with you and go over the rest of the items in the original wish list to refine the details for the final product.

    Checkpoint

  • 4

    Divide & Prioritize

    Once the items in your wish list have been refined, we help you break them out into independent work items that can each provide value to you on their own. We then work with you to prioritize them and make sure they can be done within your budget.

    Checkpoint

  • 5

    Develop & Test

    With a prioritized list of items, we set to work on developing and testing. We use a continuous delivery system which ensures that every 1-2 weeks we provide you with fully functional ready to use portions of your project.

    Checkpoint

  • 6

    Deploy (& Train)

    The best part: taking your project live. We also provide ongoing support to make sure new users can be brought up to speed and make sure that your project continues to meet your needs.

    Checkpoint
